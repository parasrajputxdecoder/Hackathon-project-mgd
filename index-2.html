😟 The Problem

Why do so many students feel lost after 12th?

The system funnels everyone into the same two paths. That leaves most students confused, pressured, and unhappy with their choices.

5%

of Indian 12th-grade students receive any formal career counseling before choosing their college or stream.

— AISHE Report, 2023
  • NEET / JEE tunnel vision

    Society treats every other career as a "backup plan," not a real choice.

  • Family & peer pressure

    What you're genuinely passionate about gets buried under what relatives expect.

  • Tier 2 & Tier 3 city gap

    Students outside metros have no career expos, no counselors, no mentors nearby.

  • Regret costs years

    Wrong choices at 17 lead to switching fields at 22 — wasted time and money.
